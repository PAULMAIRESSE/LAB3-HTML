Frequently Asked Questions

The Toronto Island Ferry Terminal is located at the foot of Bay Street at Queens Quay. The ferry ride to the Toronto Islands takes approximately 15 minutes.

It costs $8.19 for a round trip to the Island for adults, and $5.43 for children.

The best time to visit the Island is during the summer season, from May to September.

There are many activities available on the Island, such as swimming, fishing, picnicking, cycling and many more.
